About Us

Vitals and Voices is a student-led platform created to amplify the ideas, questions, and visions of the next generation of thinkers in healthcare. We believe the future of medicine depends on curious, bold minds—and we’re here to give them a place to speak up.

Whether you’re exploring medical ethics, critiquing public health policy, or just sharing a story from your volunteering experience, this site is your outlet. No credentials needed—just curiosity, honesty, and a desire to make an impact.

Our contributors are high school and college students from across the country (and world) who care about the systems that shape human well-being. Some want to be surgeons. Others public health advocates. Some are just learning. All are welcome.
