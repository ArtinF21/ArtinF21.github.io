Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit to the Youth Frontier Journal of Research?

We welcome submissions from high school and college students from anywhere in the world. You don’t need prior experience — just a passion for science and a clear, original perspective.

Do I need to pay anything to submit or get published?

No. There are no fees to submit, publish, or access articles on our site. This journal is entirely student-run and nonprofit.

Can I submit more than one piece?

Yes! You’re welcome to submit multiple pieces, either all at once or over time. Just make sure each submission follows our guidelines.

How long does the review process take?

Submissions are reviewed weekly. You’ll usually hear back from us within 7–10 days after submitting.

How do I get involved beyond submitting?

We’re always looking for new members to join the editorial team, design staff, and outreach efforts. You can email us or join our Discord to learn more.
